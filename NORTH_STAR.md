# North Star — One Assess

## Mission

Eliminate the assessment admin burden for fitness coaches forever.

## Metrics

- **Pre-launch North Star:** Days to first paying customer.
- **Post-launch North Star:** Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR).

## Targets

- **Solo coach tier:** £49/mo  
- **Studio tier:** £129/mo  
- **Reference:** 100 coaches at solo tier ≈ £4,900 MRR.

## Competitors (orientation)

- **TrueCoach** — ~£147/mo, no posture AI.
- **PTminder** — dated, no meaningful AI posture story.
- **PostureScreen** — posture-only, not full clinical assessment workflow.

## Moat

- **MediaPipe local** — zero marginal AI cost per analysis.
- **Clinical depth** — 8-phase assessment, scoring engine, coach + client reports.
- **Built by a coach** — founder-operated credibility with the ICP.

## Decision filter

Every initiative should answer: **does this move us closer to a coach paying £49/mo?**
